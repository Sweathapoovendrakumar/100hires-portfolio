## Post Content

The future of SEO is about teaching AI who you are.

A customer's journey is becoming much longer and far more fragmented. Someone might ask a question on Google, read an AI Overview, continue in AI Mode, skip your website entirely, check Reddit, ask Gemini for another opinion, look up your brand on LinkedIn or X, and only then decide whether to buy. In many cases, they may never click on a website at all.

The new challenge isn't about ranking #1 on Google. It's about making sure AI platforms understand your brand and recommend you when people ask questions.

Practical steps:
1. Keep brand information consistent across website, social media, business listings, and directories
2. Create an About page that clearly explains who you are
3. Build third-party mentions on Product Hunt, Crunchbase, LinkedIn, YouTube, Reddit, and industry directories
4. Ensure LLMs can crawl your website and structured data is in place
5. Ask an LLM what it knows about your brand — the answer reveals missing information and inconsistencies

---

## Annotation

Asking an LLM what it knows about your brand is one of the most practical and immediate AI SEO audit techniques available. It costs nothing, takes five minutes, and reveals exactly what AI systems will tell buyers about your company before they ever visit your site.
