## Key Insight

55% of AI-influenced website visits are attributed to Search in analytics. Most companies are already receiving significant AI-influenced traffic and don't know it — dramatically undervaluing their SEO investment.

---

## Post Content

SEOs, reference this study when you're asked about AI attribution. Clickstream data found that 55% of AI-influenced users come in THROUGH SEARCH.

Fascinating data study by Similarweb tracking actual user behavior 7 days after ChatGPT interaction:

1. If a brand was recommended via ChatGPT, users were 2.5x more likely to visit the site.
2. ChatGPT is used more at the beginning of the user journey — 35% were in the "Discovering" phase.
3. 55% of AI-influenced users came to brands via search, versus 40% for standard visits.
4. Users coming in through AI engage much more deeply — both Average Pageviews and Average Time on Site were basically double that of non-AI visits.

---

## Annotation

This data changes how companies should think about SEO ROI. If AI-influenced users engage 2x more deeply and are 2.5x more likely to visit after a ChatGPT recommendation, companies measuring organic search only by traffic volume are systematically underreporting the channel's contribution. The argument for continued SEO investment — even as traffic volumes decline — is supported by this data.
