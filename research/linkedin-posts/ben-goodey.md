## Key Insight

The highest-leverage SEO action for most B2B SaaS sites is not new content. It is internal links from their highest-authority existing pages to their highest-intent pages.

---

## Post Content

Simple yet powerful SEO growth tip:

1. Put your website into Semrush
2. Click on the Backlinks report
3. Click on Indexed Pages
4. This report = all your web pages with the most backlinks
5. You'll see your highest authority blog URLs
6. Add 2-3 internal links from here to other pages on your site
7. Prioritise internal links to high intent pages

This method leverages the earned authority of existing pages on your site and funnels it towards the pages you really want to rank well.

---

## Annotation

This is a B2B SaaS-specific version of PageRank sculpting — using existing domain authority rather than building new links. For 100Hires with existing blog content, this is an immediate, zero-cost SEO action. Prioritizing high-intent pages (pricing, comparison, feature pages) over informational pages reflects an understanding of B2B buying behavior that generic SEO advice misses.
