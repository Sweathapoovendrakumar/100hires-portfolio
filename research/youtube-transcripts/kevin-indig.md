---

## Why This Video

Indig conducted a structured qualitative study with 70 participants observing how real users interact with AI Overviews. The findings challenge the dominant narrative that AI is killing SEO.

---

## Key Insights Extracted

**On user behavior:** 80% of users get their final answer from classic organic results, not AI Overviews. Users click through to verify AI answers — not to discover.

**On age and trust:** Younger users engage more with AI answers. Older users skip them. Target audience age profile should inform SEO channel prioritization.

**On brand trust:** Brand perception is now an SEO variable. If users don't recognize a brand in an AI Overview, they won't click. SEO and brand teams can no longer operate separately.

**On team structure:** Best-performing companies create a surround sound effect — present authentically across multiple channels simultaneously.

**On traffic vs conversion:** Traffic is contracting. Conversion rate from organic is increasing. Companies measuring SEO by traffic volume will undervalue the channel.

---

## Relevance to 100Hires

Indig's finding that 80% of final answers still come from classic organic means 100Hires should not deprioritize traditional SEO. Optimize for AI citation AND classic organic simultaneously — the content requirements are largely the same.
