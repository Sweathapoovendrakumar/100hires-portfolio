## Why This Video

Diggity ran a 6-month AI SEO campaign for a real client. The client went from zero AI Overviews to 90+ and achieved 2,300% increase in AI referral traffic. One of the few sources in the industry with verified before/after results.

---

## Key Insights Extracted

**On content structure:** Direct, concise content that immediately answers the question gets cited. Fluffy introductions do not.

**On conversational phrasing:** AI search matches how people speak. "15-minute home workout" gets no AI Overview. "What's a quick 15-minute workout I can do at home without equipment" does.

**On headings:** H1, H2, H3 structure is how AI systems understand page architecture. A well-structured heading hierarchy is a retrieval signal, not a design preference.

**On brand trust:** LLMs learn about brands from Google Business Profile, Yelp, LinkedIn, Trustpilot. Review volume and average rating directly influence AI Overview inclusion.

**On measurement:** Tracking AI traffic requires a custom regex filter in Google Analytics 4 under referral traffic. Without this, AI-driven traffic is invisible in most dashboards.
