## Why This Video

Simmonds presents actual traffic data from CNET's AI-written content and cites Profound's research on which platforms appear most in AI Overviews — making this one of the most data-rich sources in this collection.

---

## Key Insights Extracted

**On AI-written content:** CNET's AI-written content generated 24 million annual visits and $1.3M in organic traffic value. Quality, structure, and distribution determine performance — not whether AI was involved.

**On platform citations:** YouTube and Reddit are cited most in AI Overviews. G2, Capterra, and Trust Radius traffic is declining. Companies investing in review sites while ignoring YouTube and Reddit are misallocating budgets.

**On Reddit:** Requires genuine contribution before any links. Start with branded subreddit, brand account, and helpful content across four types: educate, engage, entertain, empower.

**On repurposing:** A single piece of content reformatted for 6+ platforms using tools like ChatGPT + Canva, Descript, and 11Labs reduces distribution costs dramatically.
