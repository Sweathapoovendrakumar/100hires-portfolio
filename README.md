## What This Project Is

This is not a collection of links and transcripts.

It is a structured research base built to answer one question:

> **How are the most effective practitioners actually using AI to produce, distribute, and optimize content in 2025–2026 — and what does that mean for a B2B SaaS company like 100Hires?**

To answer that, I identified 10 expert voices across YouTube, LinkedIn, and podcasts — people who build real things and share real results, not just commentary. I collected their content, extracted the signal from the noise, and organized everything so it can directly feed a content playbook.

---

## Why AI-Powered SEO Content Production

Out of all the available topics, this one sits at the intersection of what matters most right now:

- Google Search is being restructured around AI Overviews and AI Mode
- B2B SaaS companies like 100Hires need content that gets cited by LLMs, not just ranked on Page 1
- AI tools can dramatically reduce content production costs — but only if used with judgment
- The companies winning right now are not producing the most content. They are producing the most structured, trustworthy, well-distributed content.

This topic is directly actionable for 100Hires.
